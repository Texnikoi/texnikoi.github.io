Engineering & Architecture students are encouraged to apply!

Only sophomores and above that are studying under the College of Engineering or Architecture umbrella are eligible to apply.
Here are some FAQs. Please contact us if you have other questions!

  • 01 What does Texnikoi do?

    Texnikoi participates in service, professional, and social events as a group.  

  • 02 Is there a cost to join?

    Yes, there is a one time $95 fee accepted to help cover social and involvement event costs. This fee covers the entirety of your time at Texnikoi. If this is an issue please contact us for other arrangements.

  • 03 Are there scholarships?

    Absolutely! Texnikoi gives out quite a few scholarships each year. For the 2021-2022 school year, Texnikoi dispersed over $8000 in scholarships to its 65 members. Our bylaws state that Texnikoi's scholarships given out must match social spending.
