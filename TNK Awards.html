Award Recipients

Texnikoi also recognizes OSU graduates that display qualities of leadership, integrity, and excellence as exemplified by active participation and leadership in their post graduate activities. Each year, the active membership of Texnikoi selects one alumni of the College of Engineering as a recipient of the Texnikoi Outstanding Alumni Award. This award is based upon achievements since graduation, evaluated in light of the objectives of Texnikoi. Awards for 2020 and 2021 were cancelled due to Covid. The exec board may continue this award in the near future.
